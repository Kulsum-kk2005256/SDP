What Murshidi Can Do

Murshidi is an AI-powered chatbot designed to answer questions about Qatar University

The chatbot has dual-language capabilities, i.e. it can answer questions in both English and Arabic

Murshidi is also equipped with advanced features that enable users to engage through voice input and receive spoken responses seamlessly

Compared to searching online for answers, Murshidi can deliver accurate answers far faster

Future Plans

Murshidi is currently only available online, but a mobile version for Android and iOS is in development

We plan to expand on the dual-language capability to include arabic voice chat as well

As we get to better understand what users are looking for, we plan to expand the sources of information that Murshidi gets its information from, such as the official university social media accounts or email information

The Vision

We hope that, once Murshidi becomes fully developed and functional, it can be deployed on the official Qatar University website to make it easier to access for all sorts of users, whether they are new students, prospective students, parents, long-time students, advisors, or even faculty
